# DeepZero

## 概览

DeepZero is the company represented in the source deck “AI 智能体助力决策新范式,” presented by co-founder and CEO Huang Xiaonan. In the deck, DeepZero positions its practice around AI-empowered decisions for marketing, growth, sales enablement, user operations, VOC, advertising, and AI search.
<div class="zh-trans">深演智能是《AI 智能体助力决策新范式》材料的提出方，分享人为联合创始人兼 CEO 黄晓南。材料中，深演智能将其实践定位在 AI 赋能决策，覆盖营销增长、销售赋能、用户运营、VOC、广告投放和 AI 搜索等场景。</div>

## 关键数据或事件

- The deck presents a data foundation spanning CRM data, CDP data, customer-service knowledge bases, e-commerce data, social data, ad data, retailer data, telecom data, and first-party data.
- The practice map includes AI VOC, AI consumer, AI survey, AI strategy, AI K selection, KOS, AI bidding, AI shopping guide, AI journey, AI content, AI audience selection, GAO, and GEO.
- The company frames enterprise AI agents as workflow-embedded decision systems rather than standalone chatbots.

<div class="zh-trans">关键内容包括：数据底座覆盖 CRM、CDP、客服知识库、电商、社媒、广告、零售商、运营商和一方数据；实践版图包括 AI VOC、AI 消费者、AI 问卷、AI 策略、AI 选K、KOS、AI 投手、AI 导购、AI 旅程、AI 内容、AI 圈人、GAO 和 GEO；公司将企业级 AI Agent 定位为嵌入业务工作流的决策系统，而非独立聊天机器人。</div>
