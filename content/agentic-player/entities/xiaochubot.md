# 小厨bot

## 概览

小厨bot 是润典企业智能体解决方案中的产品实体，定位为餐饮门店与营运团队的企微智能助手。它把日常操作、知识查询、预警提醒和任务执行收敛到一个聊天式入口里。

## 关键数据或事件

- The deck presents 小厨bot as the primary interface for store operations, including ordering, stock checks, sold-out handling, reconciliation, repairs, and knowledge lookup.
- It supports private deployment, WeCom-native interaction, and a staged evolution from decision support to intelligent alerting and autonomous optimization.
- The underlying implementation combines LLM, RAG, MCP, Skills, H5 cards, backend services, and business-system APIs.
