# Agentic Marketing OS

## 定义

Agentic Marketing OS is an AI-agent operating system for marketing and growth. It connects data foundations, brand knowledge, consumer insight, content generation, media optimization, sales enablement, CRM journeys, VOC, product innovation, and AI search visibility into a coordinated decision-and-execution layer.
<div class="zh-trans">Agentic Marketing OS 是面向营销与增长的 AI Agent 操作系统。它把数据底座、品牌知识、消费者洞察、内容生成、投放优化、销售赋能、CRM 旅程、VOC、产品创新和 AI 搜索可见度连接成统一的决策与执行层。</div>

## 运作方式

The operating system starts from integrated data: CRM, CDP, customer-service knowledge, e-commerce, social, advertising, retailer, telecom, and first-party data. Agents then support scenarios such as AI VOC, AI consumer simulation, AI survey, user insight, product insight, trend insight, concept testing, social marketing, AI strategy, AI KOL selection, KOS, attribution, AI bidding, sales enablement, customer-service QA, AI shopping guide, smart journey, AI tags, AI content, and AI audience selection.
<div class="zh-trans">该操作系统以整合数据为起点，包括 CRM、CDP、客服知识库、电商、社媒、广告、零售商、运营商和一方数据。Agent 在此基础上支持 AI VOC、AI 消费者模拟、AI 问卷、用户洞察、产品洞察、趋势洞察、概念测试、Social 营销、AI 策略、AI 选K、KOS、归因、AI 投手、销售赋能、客服质检、AI 导购、智能旅程、AI 标签、AI 内容和 AI 圈人等场景。</div>

It changes marketing from a human-capacity model to a decision-system model. Instead of relying on manual segmentation, intuition-based content strategy, surface-level KOL selection, and disconnected attribution, the system uses data and agents to generate strategies, content, journeys, optimization actions, and feedback loops.
<div class="zh-trans">它把营销从“人力产能模型”转向“决策系统模型”。传统营销依赖人工圈人、经验型内容策略、表层达人选择和割裂归因；Agentic Marketing OS 则用数据和 Agent 生成策略、内容、旅程、优化动作和反馈闭环。</div>

## 为什么重要

For CMOs and growth leaders, the strategic value is not only efficiency. The larger shift is that marketing decisions become faster, more personalized, more testable, and more connected across the full funnel from insight to execution to attribution.
<div class="zh-trans">对 CMO 和增长负责人而言，其战略价值不只是提效。更大的变化是营销决策变得更快、更个性化、更可测试，并且能贯通从洞察、执行到归因的全链路。</div>
