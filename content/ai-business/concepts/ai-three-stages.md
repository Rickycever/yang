# AI 商业进化三阶段

## Definition

A framework (attributed to 陈天桥 and popularized by the GEA keynote) describing how AI's impact on commercial organizations evolves across three stages. As of early 2026, most enterprises are in Stage 1, some advanced companies (like Anker) are in Stage 2, and Stage 3 remains conceptual.

<div class="zh-trans">一个描述AI对商业组织影响如何跨三个阶段演进的框架（归功于陈天桥，由GEA主题演讲推广）。截至2026年初，大多数企业处于第一阶段，一些先进公司（如安克）处于第二阶段，第三阶段仍为概念阶段。</div>

## How It Works

| Stage | Name | Characteristics | Human Role |
|-------|------|-----------------|------------|
| 1 | **AI Empowered** | Copilot mode — AI assists every task | Primary executor, AI assists |
| 2 | **AI Native** | AI autonomously drives work outputs | Judgment provider, not main executor |
| 3 | **AI Awakened** | Unknown form; AI-native commercial entity | To be defined |

This maps to OTGO's own evolution: lobster agents (Stage 2), octo platform (enabling Stage 2→3 for enterprises).

<div class="zh-trans">三个阶段：1.AI赋能（Copilot模式，AI辅助每项任务，人是主执行者）；2.AI原生（AI自动驱动工作产出，人提供判断而非主力执行）；3.AI觉醒（形态未知的AI原生商业体，人的角色待定义）。映射到OTGO：龙虾Agent（第二阶段），octo平台（帮助企业从第二阶段向第三阶段演进）。</div>

**Parallel technical paradigm shift (3 years):**
- 2023: Generative AI (can generate)
- 2024: Reasoning AI (can think, GPT-4o)
- 2025: Agentic AI (can act, Claude Code)

The commercial stages roughly lag the technical stages by 12–18 months.

<div class="zh-trans">并行的技术范式转变（3年）：2023生成式AI（能生成）→2024推理AI（能思考，GPT-4o）→2025智能体AI（能干活，Claude Code）。商业阶段大约滞后技术阶段12-18个月。</div>

## Why It Matters

The framework provides a shared vocabulary for enterprise AI strategy conversations and helps organizations self-diagnose where they are. Critically, it establishes that Stage 2 (AI Native) already exists — it's not a future aspiration but an achievable state (evidenced by Anker's 2025 Phase 4 jump).

<div class="zh-trans">该框架为企业AI战略对话提供了共同词汇，帮助组织自我诊断所处位置。关键点：第二阶段（AI原生）已经存在——不是未来愿景而是可实现状态（安克2025年第四阶段跳跃为证）。</div>
