# 主观世界模型 (Subjective World Model)

## Definition

A Subjective World Model is an AI-powered simulation of a consumer's mental model and decision-making process, built from four data dimensions: Question (social media expression), Story (deep interview rationale), Cognition (psychological models), and Behavior (behavioral data). The result is an AI Persona that simulates how a real person thinks, feels, and decides — available on-demand, at any scale.

<div class="zh-trans">主观世界模型是基于四个数据维度构建的消费者心智模型和决策过程的AI驱动仿真：Question（社媒表达）、Story（深度访谈原因）、Cognition（心理模型）、Behavior（行为数据）。结果是一个AI人设，模拟真实人物的思考、感受和决策——按需可用，规模任意。</div>

## How It Works

**Traditional user research vs Subjective World Model:**

| Dimension | Traditional | Subjective World Model |
|-----------|------------|----------------------|
| Scale | 2,000 person survey, 3–6 months | 12M person AI interviews in days |
| Availability | One-time, scheduled | On-demand, 24/7 |
| Coverage | Accessible demographics | Any user (including hard-to-reach professionals) |
| Accuracy | ~90%+ (real people) | ~85% (AI Persona for real users) |
| Reusability | None | Personas can be reused, grouped, queried |

**Applications:**
- Dairy brand: replaced 2,000-person survey (3–6 months) with 60% AI + 40% real people
- Electric tool brand: AI Personas simulated hard-to-reach users (doctors, financial professionals)
- US professor: AI interviewed 120,000 Chinese adults (20K families) on fertility; 30 seconds per transcript; AI Personas grouped into real-time focus groups

<div class="zh-trans">应用案例：乳制品品牌（2000人调研3-6个月→60% AI+40%真人大幅提速）；电动工具品牌（AI模拟难触达的专业用户）；美国教授（AI访谈12万中国人，30秒生成逐字稿，AI人设随时组焦点小组）。</div>

## Why It Matters

The Subjective World Model enables "always-on user research" — continuous, scalable consumer intelligence rather than periodic surveys. For product innovation, this means AI can run background research 7×24 and generate ideas before humans even ask for them. For marketing, each campaign content piece can be tested against AI Personas before launch.

<div class="zh-trans">主观世界模型实现了"始终在线的用户研究"——持续可扩展的消费者洞察，而非周期性调研。对产品创新而言，AI可以7×24后台运行调研并在人提问前就生成创意。对营销而言，每条内容在发布前都可以对AI人设进行测试。</div>
