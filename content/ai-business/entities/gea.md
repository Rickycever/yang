# GEA / 特赞 (Generative Enterprise Architecture)

## Overview

GEA (Generative Enterprise Architecture) is an enterprise AI platform company, associated with 特赞 (Tezign) and its founder 范凌. GEA focuses on helping enterprises build enterprise-grade agent systems centered on context accumulation, general-purpose agents, and creative reasoning for marketing and product innovation use cases.

<div class="zh-trans">GEA（通用企业级智能体架构）是一家企业AI平台公司，与特赞及其创始人范凌相关。GEA专注于帮助企业构建以上下文积累、通用Agent和创意推理为核心的企业级智能体系统，主要面向营销和产品创新场景。</div>

## Role

GEA positions itself as a "Full Stack AI" provider — not just selling tools but running entire business workflows end-to-end with AI, demonstrating ROI first, then helping client organizations transition their teams. Key differentiator: they use AI to run the client's campaign before asking the client to adopt the workflow internally.

<div class="zh-trans">GEA定位为"Full Stack AI"提供商——不只销售工具，而是用AI端到端运行整个业务工作流，先展示ROI，再帮助客户组织转变其团队。关键差异化：他们先用AI替客户跑通活动流程，再要求客户在内部采用该工作流。</div>

## Key Actions / Decisions

- Developed the Subjective World Model framework for AI-powered user research
- Created the GEA Architecture (one general-purpose agent + context + skills + orchestration)
- Introduced the Creative Reasoning Model for business divergent thinking
- Case results: 7× ROI improvement, 40% cost savings for international snack brand new product launch

<div class="zh-trans">GEA开发了主观世界模型框架（AI驱动用户研究）；创建了GEA架构（通用Agent+上下文+技能+编排）；引入创意推理模型（商业发散思维）；案例结果：国际小吃品牌新品ROI提升7倍，节省40%成本。</div>
