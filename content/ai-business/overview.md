# AI 商业进化 (AI Business Evolution)

AI is reshaping how enterprises create customers and compete. As of Q1 2026, the decisive battleground has shifted from model capability to **enterprise context accumulation**. This topic documents the frameworks, cases, and architectural patterns emerging from that transition.

<div class="zh-trans">AI正在重塑企业创造顾客和竞争的方式。截至2026年Q1，决定性战场已从模型能力转移到**企业上下文积累**。本话题记录了这一转变中涌现的框架、案例和架构模式。</div>
