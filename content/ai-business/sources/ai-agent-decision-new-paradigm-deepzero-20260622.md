# AI 智能体助力决策新范式

This source is a 34-page presentation by Huang Xiaonan, co-founder and CEO of DeepZero, on how enterprise AI agents are becoming a new paradigm for growth and decision-making. The deck positions AI agents as business workflow systems rather than chatbots, with the highest-value applications in marketing, sales enablement, CRM operations, VOC, product innovation, advertising, and AI search optimization.
<div class="zh-trans">本来源是一份由深演智能联合创始人兼 CEO 黄晓南分享的 34 页演示材料，主题是企业级 AI 智能体如何成为增长与决策的新范式。材料将 AI Agent 定位为业务工作流系统，而不是简单聊天机器人，核心应用集中在营销、销售赋能、CRM 运营、VOC、产品创新、广告投放和 AI 搜索优化等高价值场景。</div>

## 摘要

The presentation starts from the rapid evolution of foundation models and the explosive adoption of AI agents in 2025. It cites external signals that 79% of organizations have adopted AI agents to some degree, and that 40% of enterprise applications may integrate task-specific agents by 2026. However, the deck stresses that enterprise agents remain difficult because LLMs are probabilistic and suffer from hallucination, weak vertical data, insufficient memory, engineering integration limits, and lack of deep business knowledge.
<div class="zh-trans">材料从基础模型快速演进和 2025 年 AI Agent 大规模涌现切入，引用外部信号指出 79% 的组织已经在某种程度上采用 AI Agent，且到 2026 年 40% 的企业应用可能集成面向特定任务的 Agent。但材料强调，企业级 Agent 仍然很难，因为 LLM 本质上是概率系统，存在幻觉、垂直数据不足、记忆不足、工程接口限制和业务知识融合不深等问题。</div>

The proposed answer is a shift from purely probabilistic LLM behavior to deterministic business architecture. Instead of pursuing full autonomy immediately, enterprises should embed agents into the most important workflows, keep humans at the helm, and use AI to handle repetitive work while humans supervise key nodes, review outputs, and handle exceptions.
<div class="zh-trans">材料提出的答案，是从纯概率式 LLM 行为转向确定性的业务架构。企业不应一开始追求完全自动化，而应把 Agent 嵌入最关键的业务工作流，让人类执掌关键节点，由 AI 处理繁琐重复工作，人类负责监督、评审和复杂例外处理。</div>

## 核心要点

### 1. Enterprise agents need deterministic architecture, not only stronger models

The deck argues that LLMs cannot complete the last mile by themselves because they generate probabilistic outputs. The intelligence of an agent depends on the fidelity of the data and context it can access. Enterprise reliability therefore comes from architecture: RAG, vector databases, MCP, Function Call, Skills, ReAct-style reasoning, expert small models, and engineering integration.
<div class="zh-trans">材料认为，LLM 自身无法完成企业落地“最后一公里”，因为它输出的是概率结果。Agent 的智能程度取决于它能获取的数据和上下文保真度。因此，企业级可靠性来自架构：RAG、向量数据库、MCP、Function Call、Skills、ReAct 协同推理、专家知识小模型以及工程集成。</div>

### 2. Human-in-the-loop evolves into Human-at-the-helm

The deck distinguishes between humans merely being inserted into AI workflows and humans actively steering the system. Human-at-the-helm means AI executes repetitive work autonomously within boundaries, while humans supervise key decision points, evaluate outputs, and handle complex exceptions.
<div class="zh-trans">材料区分了“人类在环”和“人类执掌”。Human-at-the-helm 不是简单把人插入流程，而是让 AI 在边界内自主处理重复工作，人类在关键决策点进行监督、评审和复杂例外处理。</div>

### 3. DeepZero's practice map covers the full marketing-growth operating system

The deck maps enterprise AI agents across data foundations and business scenarios: CRM data, CDP data, customer-service knowledge bases, e-commerce data, social data, ad data, retailer data, and telecom data feed into use cases such as AI VOC, AI consumer, AI survey, user insight, product insight, trend insight, concept testing, social marketing, AI strategy, AI K selection, KOS, attribution, AI bidding, sales enablement, customer-service QA, AI shopping guide, channel insight, GTM, smart journey, AI tags, AI content, and AI audience selection.
<div class="zh-trans">材料展示了深演智能的企业 AI Agent 实践版图：以 CRM 数据、CDP 数据、客服知识库、电商数据、社媒数据、广告投放数据、零售商数据、运营商数据等为数据底座，覆盖 AI VOC、AI 消费者、AI 问卷、用户洞察、新品洞察、趋势洞察、概念测试、Social 营销、AI 策略、AI 选K、KOS、全域归因、AI 投手、销售赋能、客服质检、AI 导购、渠道洞察、GTM、智能旅程、AI 标签、AI 内容和 AI 圈人等场景。</div>

### 4. Sales enablement shifts toward one-person-one-strategy conversion

In sales enablement, the agent combines AI Persona, CDP tags, conversation data, recommendation algorithms, knowledge bases, script libraries, SOP templates, and counterexample libraries. The goal is to generate a personalized follow-up strategy for each customer, including content preferences, touchpoint preferences, active time, decision style, promotion sensitivity, value messaging, proof points, and risk-avoidance messaging.
<div class="zh-trans">在销售赋能场景中，Agent 结合 AI Persona、CDP 标签、企微与客服对话、推荐算法、知识库、话术库、SOP 模板和反例库，为每个客户生成“一人一策”的跟进策略，覆盖内容偏好、触点偏好、活跃时段、决策类型、促销敏感度、功能价值话术、场景价值话术、案例证明话术和风险规避话术。</div>

### 5. Social marketing becomes an AI plus data decision system

The deck describes the pain points of traditional social marketing: experience-driven strategy, slow content production, shallow KOL selection data, manual media optimization, disconnected conversion data, and human-capacity bottlenecks. The AI-enabled approach uses trend data, competitor monitoring, content-feature data, topic tags, creator-account data, creator-content data, real-time spend, attribution data, first-party data, and e-commerce data to support strategy, content strategy, creator strategy, media optimization, and attribution.
<div class="zh-trans">材料指出传统 Social 营销的痛点包括策略依赖经验直觉、内容慢工细活、达人选择依赖表层数据、投流手动调优、转化链路割裂和人力产能瓶颈。AI 赋能路径则把大盘趋势、竞品监控、内容特征、话题标签、达人账号、达人内容、实时消耗、投放链路、一方数据和电商数据整合起来，支持整体策略、内容策略、达人策略、投放和归因决策。</div>

### 6. CRM operations move from manual segmentation to intelligent journey generation

For CRM and user operations, the deck frames the traditional workflow as manual audience selection, manual analysis, manual content generation, and delayed canvas execution. The AI workflow generates personalized multi-touchpoint content, supports A/B testing, and connects generated content directly to marketing workflow deployment. Smart journey generation combines data and knowledge retrieval, strategy-factor analysis, campaign planning, small-model prediction, and journey generation.
<div class="zh-trans">在 CRM 与用户运营中，材料将传统流程概括为人工圈人、人工分析、人工内容和画布执行滞后。AI 流程可以生成多触点个性化内容，支持 A/B 测试，并将内容直接部署到营销触点工作流。智能旅程生成则结合数据与知识检索、策略因子分析、营销活动规划、小模型预测和旅程生成。</div>

### 7. VOC and AI consumers become product innovation infrastructure

The deck positions AI VOC as a deep product, operations, and user-insight engine that can analyze user reviews, social content and comments, WeCom conversations, customer service conversations, complaints, tickets, and operational feedback. AI consumers simulate audience groups for deep interviews, survey research, qualitative analysis, quantitative statistics, and action recommendations.
<div class="zh-trans">材料将 AI VOC 定位为深度产品、运营和用户洞察引擎，可分析用户评价、社媒内容与评论、企微对话、客服对话、客诉、工单和运营反馈。AI 消费者则用于模拟目标人群，支持深度访谈、问卷调研、定性分析、定量统计和行动建议。</div>

### 8. AI search changes the marketing logic from traffic thinking to answer thinking

The deck argues that AI tools have become a new hub for consumers acquiring brand information. It cites a 2026 China AI user behavior and attitude study showing AI tools at 52.8%, compared with traditional search engines at 37.0%, for brand information acquisition. The new optimization model combines GAO, GEO, and AI user behavior research to improve how brands appear in model-generated answers.
<div class="zh-trans">材料认为，AI 工具正在成为消费者获取品牌信息的新枢纽。其引用《2026 中国 AI 用户行为与态度研究》显示，消费者获取品牌信息时 AI 工具占 52.8%，传统搜索引擎为 37.0%。新的优化模式结合 GAO、GEO 和 AI 用户行为研究，提升品牌在大模型生成答案中的表现。</div>

### 9. Enterprise agents evolve through five phases

The deck proposes a five-phase evolution path: tool replacement, workflow collaboration, workflow autonomy, goal autonomy, and business partnership. The technical base evolves from LLM content generation, to LLM plus Workflow process understanding, to agent architecture task execution, to RL plus simulation KPI responsibility, and finally to multi-agent strategic co-creation.
<div class="zh-trans">材料提出企业级 Agent 的五阶段演进路径：工具替代、流程协作、流程自治、目标自治、业务合伙人。技术底座从 LLM 内容生成，演进到 LLM + Workflow 的流程理解，再到 Agent 架构的任务执行，进一步到 RL + 仿真支撑 KPI 负责，最终到多 Agent 战略共创。</div>
