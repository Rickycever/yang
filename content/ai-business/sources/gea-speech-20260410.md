# GEA 演讲：智能体驱动的商业进化

## Summary

A keynote at an industry forum by a GEA (Generative Enterprise Architecture) representative, systematically covering the AI technology evolution path (3 years), three stages of commercial AI impact, and GEA's enterprise agent architecture. Key insight: the competition is not about which agent, but about who builds the richer **enterprise context system**.

<div class="zh-trans">GEA（通用企业级智能体架构）代表在行业论坛的主题演讲，系统阐述AI技术三年演进路径、AI对商业的三阶段影响，以及GEA的企业级智能体架构。核心洞见：竞争不在于有哪个Agent，而在于谁建立了更丰富的**企业上下文系统**。</div>

## Key Points

**AI technical evolution — 3 paradigms in 3 years:**
- 2023: Generative AI — AI can generate (text/image/audio/video)
- 2024: Reasoning AI — AI can think and solve problems (GPT-4o, GPT-o1)
- 2025: Agentic AI — AI can act, use tools, execute code (Claude Code)

<div class="zh-trans">AI技术三年三范式：2023生成式AI（能生成）→2024推理式AI（能思考解题）→2025智能体AI（能干活、用工具、执行代码）。</div>

**Three stages of commercial impact (from 陈天桥's framework):**
1. **AI Empowered**: Copilot mode, AI assists every task. Most enterprises are here now.
2. **AI Native**: AI autonomously drives work outputs, humans provide judgment.
3. **AI Awakened**: No concrete form yet; predicted to emerge in the next year.

<div class="zh-trans">商业影响三阶段（引用陈天桥框架）：AI赋能（Copilot模式，当前主流）→AI原生（AI自动驱动产出，人负责判断）→AI觉醒（尚无成型案例，预计明年出现）。</div>

**Drucker anchor**: The only purpose of a business is to create a customer. Only two functions produce results: Marketing and Innovation. All Agent Business should ultimately serve these three objectives: user discovery, Marketing, Innovation.

<div class="zh-trans">德鲁克锚点：企业唯一目的是"创造顾客"。只有Marketing和Innovation两个功能产生结果。所有Agent Business最终服务三个目标：发明用户、营销、产品创新。</div>

**GEA Architecture — one general-purpose agent + context + skills:**
The old consensus (6 months ago): one specialized agent per role (HR Agent, Marketing Agent). The new consensus: one general-purpose agent (that can write code) + industry skills + enterprise context. Context is the competitive moat: structured data (single source of truth) + unstructured data (images, video, decisions) = the enterprise's world model.

<div class="zh-trans">GEA架构——一个通用Agent+上下文+技能：旧共识（6个月前）：每个岗位一个专用Agent。新共识：一个通用Agent（会写代码的）+行业技能+企业上下文。上下文是竞争护城河：结构化数据（唯一真相）+非结构化数据（图文视频+决策逻辑）=企业的世界模型。</div>

**Counter-intuitive finding**: Lead Agent doesn't need the most advanced model; sub-agents (executing) must use the strongest models. "The leader doesn't have to be the smartest — the workers do."

<div class="zh-trans">反直觉发现：Lead Agent不需要最先进的模型；Sub-Agent（执行层）必须用最强模型。"领导不必太聪明，干活的人要机灵。"</div>

**Exploration vs Exploitation** (James March 1991): AI strengthens exploitation (optimization); but businesses need exploration (testing uncertain directions). AI's value: dramatically lowers exploration cost, enabling parallel testing of 5 strategies instead of 1.

<div class="zh-trans">探索与利用（James March 1991）：AI强化利用（优化），但企业需要探索（测试不确定方向）。AI的价值：大幅降低探索成本，让并行测试5种策略变得可负担。</div>
