## Key Points

**Subjective World Model (主观世界模型):**
4 data dimensions — Question (social media expression), Story (deep interview), Cognition (psychological models), Behavior (behavioral data). AI Persona accuracy for real users: ~85%, available on-demand.

Case: US professor AI-interviewed 120,000 Chinese young adults (20K families) on fertility decisions. 30 seconds per transcript. AI Personas can be grouped into a "focus group" and queried anytime.

<div class="zh-trans">主观世界模型：4个数据维度——Question（社媒表达）、Story（深度访谈原因）、Cognition（心理模型）、Behavior（行为数据）。AI人设对真实用户的模拟准确率约85%，随时可调用。案例：美国教授用AI访谈了12万中国年轻人（2万家庭）的生育意愿，30秒生成逐字稿，AI Persona可随时拉群做焦点讨论。</div>

**Product innovation — AI as the 60-point generator:**
AI runs 7×24 collecting macro market signals + micro user feedback, auto-generating product ideas. Humans focus exclusively on taking ideas from 60 points to 100+. Case: 麻将巧克力 — AI ran the complete pipeline from signal to concept to packaging; team only made final judgment calls.

<div class="zh-trans">产品创新——AI作为60分生成器：AI 7×24收集宏观市场信号+微观用户反馈，自动生成产品创意。人专注于把创意从60分提升到100分以上。案例：麻将巧克力——AI跑通从信号到概念到包装的完整链路，团队只做最终判断。</div>

**Marketing matrix — 5 AI roles:**
① Content understanding + tagging; ② Script evaluation model; ③ Attention model (multi-objective optimization); ④ Account persona management (each account has distinct identity); ⑤ Schedule optimization for max influence. Case: flip phone brand built hundreds of overseas accounts → 1.2B impressions target met.

<div class="zh-trans">营销矩阵——AI的5个角色：①内容理解+拆分打标；②脚本评价模型；③注意力模型（多目标优化）；④账号人设管理（每个账号有独立个性）；⑤排期优化最大化影响力。案例：翻盖手机品牌在海外建立数百账号，达成12亿曝光目标。</div>

**Full Stack deployment methodology:**
Problem: most enterprises stuck at POC validation. Solution: GEA runs the entire business cycle end-to-end as AI; humans play Judgment role. First demonstrate ROI, then migrate existing team workflows. Case: international snack brand new product — 40% cost savings, 7× ROI improvement.

<div class="zh-trans">Full Stack落地方法论：问题：多数企业停留在POC阶段。解法：GEA作为AI端到端运行整个业务链路；人扮演Judgment角色。先展示ROI，再逐步迁移现有团队工作方式。案例：国际小吃品牌新品——节省40%成本，ROI提升7倍。</div>
