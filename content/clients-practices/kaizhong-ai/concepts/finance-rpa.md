# Finance RPA

## 定义

Finance RPA is the automation of repetitive finance and order-processing tasks across customer platforms, internal ERP systems, email, and structured reports. In Kaizhong's project, it addresses the monthly workload created by more than 50 customer platforms, multiple document formats, and different customer-specific processing rules.
<div class="zh-trans">Finance RPA 是对跨客户平台、内部 ERP、邮件和结构化报表的重复性财务与订单处理工作进行自动化。在凯众项目中，它主要应对 50+ 客户平台、多种单据格式和不同客户处理规则带来的月度工作负担。</div>

## 运作方式

The automation flow covers customer-platform login, settlement statement retrieval, element extraction, part mapping, data transformation, settlement ledger creation, settlement and receivable document creation in Kingdee, invoice distribution, forecast and production-plan retrieval, forecast document creation, revenue accrual, cost accrual, and contract-element recognition.
<div class="zh-trans">自动化流程覆盖客户平台开户、结算单获取、要素提取、零件映射、数据转换、结算单台账生成、金蝶结算单和应收单创建、发票分发、预测单和排产计划获取、预测单生成、收入预提、成本预提以及合同要素识别。</div>

Human staff remain responsible for order confirmation, exception handling, special-document processing, and risk handling. This positions RPA as a transaction automation layer rather than a full replacement for financial control.
<div class="zh-trans">人工仍负责订单确认、异常单据处理、特殊单据处理和风险处理。因此，RPA 在这里是事务自动化层，而不是对财务管控职能的完全替代。</div>

## 为什么重要

The management value is clear because the work is high-frequency, rules-driven, and measurable. The deck targets settlement processing reduction from 5 days to 1 day per month and order recognition reduction from 22 days to 2 days per month, while also reducing error rates and freeing staff from low-value manual handling.
<div class="zh-trans">该场景的管理价值清晰，因为相关工作高频、规则驱动且容易量化。材料目标是将结算单处理从每月 5 天降至 1 天，将订单识别从每月 22 天降至 2 天，同时降低错误率，把员工从低价值手工处理中释放出来。</div>
