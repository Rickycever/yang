# Digiwin Digital Intelligence

## 概览

Digiwin Digital Intelligence is the implementation and presentation provider for the Kaizhong AI project deck. The source material is credited to Digiwin Digital Intelligence Customer Success Center and presents the AI implementation path and expected benefits for Kaizhong.
<div class="zh-trans">鼎捷数智是凯众 AI 项目材料中的实施与方案提供方。原始资料署名为鼎捷数智客户成功中心，内容展示了凯众 AI 项目的实施路径和预期效益。</div>

In this project, Digiwin's role is positioned around manufacturing AI implementation: R&D demand parsing, drawing-understanding models, knowledge base construction, and finance automation workflows connected to systems such as Kingdee and customer platforms.
<div class="zh-trans">在该项目中，鼎捷数智的角色围绕制造业 AI 落地展开，包括研发需求拆解、图纸理解模型、知识库建设，以及连接金蝶和客户平台的财务自动化流程。</div>

## 关键数据或事件

- Presented the project journey from 2025-06 to 2025-11.
- Designed the R&D AI architecture with multiple agents and model capabilities.
- Proposed finance RPA targets including settlement processing from 5 days to 1 day and order recognition from 22 days to 2 days per month.

<div class="zh-trans">关键事项包括：展示 2025 年 6 月至 11 月的项目历程；设计包含多个 Agent 和模型能力的研发 AI 架构；提出财务 RPA 目标，包括结算处理从每月 5 天降至 1 天、订单识别从每月 22 天降至 2 天。</div>
