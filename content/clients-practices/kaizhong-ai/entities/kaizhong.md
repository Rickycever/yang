# Kaizhong

## 概览

Kaizhong is an automotive component manufacturer whose stated vision is to become a leading and globalized technology enterprise in its industry. Its mission is to provide safety and comfort for every trip, with values including integrity, sharing, innovation, collaboration, and efficiency.
<div class="zh-trans">凯众是一家汽车零部件制造企业，企业愿景是成为行业领先的、全球化的科技企业。其使命是“为每一次出行提供安全与舒适”，核心价值观包括正道、共享、创新、协作和高效。</div>

The company operates across R&D and manufacturing scenarios with high requirements for design precision, product quality, and supply-chain responsiveness. Its product categories include plastic damping parts, isolator protection, top mounts, jounce bumpers, accelerator pedal modules, pedals, and callipers.
<div class="zh-trans">凯众业务覆盖研发和制造场景，对设计精度、产品质量和供应链响应效率要求较高。产品包括减震塑料件、隔震垫、顶支撑、缓冲块、电子加速踏板、制动及加速踏板和卡钳等。</div>

## 关键数据或事件

- Founded 25+ years ago.
- Annual sales: 8.4 billion RMB.
- Group employees: 700+.
- Domestic sites include Shanghai R&D Center, Luoyang, Chongqing, Nantong, Ningde, and Guangzhou.
- Overseas footprint includes Detroit, Guanajuato, Frankfurt, Manchester, Czech logistics, and Morocco.
