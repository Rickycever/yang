# 凯众 AI 项目全景概览

Kaizhong's AI project is a manufacturing-sector AI transformation initiative centered on R&D efficiency, enterprise knowledge reuse, and finance/order automation. The project frames AI not only as a productivity tool, but also as a way to improve employee well-being through workload reduction, capability enablement, and knowledge support.
<div class="zh-trans">凯众 AI 项目是一个面向制造业场景的 AI 转型项目，核心围绕研发效率、企业知识复用、财务与订单自动化展开。该项目不仅把 AI 视为提效工具，也将其定位为通过减负、赋能和知识支持提升员工幸福感的管理抓手。</div>

## 摘要

The source deck presents three layers of value: R&D AI for demand parsing, similar-product retrieval, drawing understanding, and design review; a role-based R&D knowledge base for controlled knowledge accumulation; and finance RPA for settlement statement processing, forecast/order plan handling, invoice distribution, and accrual automation.
<div class="zh-trans">该资料呈现三层价值：研发 AI 用于需求拆解、相似产品检索、图纸理解和设计评审；基于角色权限的研发知识库用于知识沉淀与管控；财务 RPA 用于结算单处理、预测单/排产计划处理、发票分发和预提自动化。</div>

The business case is built around measurable efficiency gains: demand response efficiency is expected to improve by more than 70%, engineering changes are expected to fall by more than 40%, settlement processing is expected to shrink from 5 days to 1 day per month, and order recognition work from 22 days to 2 days per month.
<div class="zh-trans">其商业价值围绕可量化效率收益展开：需求响应效率预计提升 70% 以上，工程变更预计减少 40% 以上，结算单处理从每月 5 天压缩到 1 天，订单识别工作从每月 22 天压缩到 2 天。</div>
