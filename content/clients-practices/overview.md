# Clients Practices 全景概览

Clients Practices is a case library for enterprise AI and digital transformation implementations. It focuses on real client contexts, implementation paths, measurable outcomes, and reusable delivery patterns.
<div class="zh-trans">Clients Practices 是企业 AI 与数字化转型落地案例库，重点沉淀真实客户场景、实施路径、可量化收益和可复用交付模式。</div>

## 摘要

This topic organizes client cases by company and project. Each case keeps its own overview, source pages, concept pages, entity pages, and synthesis pages when needed. The purpose is to support future sales, solution design, and management discussions with concrete implementation evidence rather than abstract product claims.
<div class="zh-trans">该主题按客户公司与项目组织案例。每个案例保留独立的 overview、source、concept、entity，必要时补充 synthesis。其目的是为后续销售、方案设计和管理层沟通提供具体落地证据，而不是停留在抽象产品能力描述。</div>
