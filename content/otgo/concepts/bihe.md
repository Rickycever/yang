# 闭环 (Closed-Loop) — OTGO's Core Strategic Filter

"闭环" (closed-loop) is OTGO's single most important strategic criterion. It means: a business initiative produces a **complete, self-reinforcing data cycle** — the output of one stage feeds directly back as input to improve the next cycle. If a business cannot generate a closed loop, OTGO will not invest in it, and investors (such as IDG) will not fund it.

<div class="zh-trans">"闭环"是 OTGO 最核心的战略判断标准：一项业务必须能产生**完整的自增强数据循环**——某一阶段的输出直接反馈为下一轮的输入改善。无法形成闭环的业务，OTGO 不投入，投资人（如 IDG）也不会投。</div>

---

## Definition

A closed loop requires two things:
1. **Complete context input:** The person who holds the real requirement provides their full context directly to the AI — no human middlemen filtering or summarizing.
2. **Measurable feedback:** The outcome of the AI's work is captured, evaluated, and fed back to improve the next iteration.

The loop: **Requirement → AI action → Measurable output → Evaluation → Better next requirement.**

<div class="zh-trans">闭环需要两件事：① 完整 context 输入：真正有需求的人将全部信息直接输入 AI，无人类中间层；② 可量化反馈：AI 工作的结果被捕捉、评估，并反馈以改善下一次迭代。循环：需求 → AI 行动 → 可量化输出 → 评估 → 更好的下次需求。</div>

## How to Apply It

### Business Evaluation Test

Ask one question: **"Can this business produce a closed loop?"**

If the answer is no, or "not for a while," stop. Do not continue.

> "不形成闭环，在这个时代就死了，就等着被抛弃就行了。" — 吴明辉

### Closed vs. Open Examples

**Closeable loops:**
| Business | Loop Mechanism |
|----------|---------------|
| E-commerce content | AI creates → customer buys/doesn't → conversion data feeds back → better content |
| vivo media campaign | AI generates content → posts → platform analytics → AI optimizes next batch |
| 顺丰 marketing | Social listening → AI content → ad placement → performance data → better listening |
| octo custom dev | Client need → lobster builds → client uses → usage data → better lobster |
| KOL risk screening | Risk check → client feedback on accuracy → better risk model |

**Hard to close (and OTGO's workarounds):**
| Business | Problem | Workaround |
|----------|---------|------------|
| KV (brand visual identity) | Top-down enterprise decisions, long cycles, hard to quantify | Use IDEF (content evaluation tool) to add a quantitative feedback node |

### The IDG Investor Story

Two partners discussed for two hours. Final question: "Can KV generate a closed loop?" Answer: "Not for a while." Response: "Then we won't invest." — This shows the criterion is not OTGO-specific; it reflects how the market now evaluates AI businesses.

<div class="zh-trans">IDG 两位合伙人聊了两小时，最后只问一个问题："KV 这个业务能产生闭环吗？"答案是"一时半会产生不了"，直接不投。这说明该标准不是 OTGO 独有，而是市场评估 AI 业务的普遍标准。</div>

## Why It Matters

### For AI Systems

A closed loop is the mechanism by which AI systems actually improve over time rather than staying static. Without it, you have a tool. With it, you have a compounding asset.

### For Humans in the Loop

The shift in human value: previously, humans explored and summarized to form conclusions. Now, humans set requirements, AI explores, humans verify the output. **The person who sets goals is the most valuable person.** Information relayers have no value.

<div class="zh-trans">人的价值转变：以前人去探索、人来总结形成结论；现在人提要求、AI 探索、人验收。**提目标的人是最宝贵的。** 做信息传话的人没有价值。</div>

### For OTGO as a Company

Closed-loop determines what OTGO builds, sells, and invests in. It filters out projects that look impressive but don't compound. It protects against building things that IDG-level investors will ignore.

## The Knowledge Management Loop

The 天合光能 discussion added a manufacturing-specific version:

> Knowledge guides roles → roles guide actions → actions produce retrospectives → retrospectives distill experience → experience enriches knowledge → loop

This is the same closed-loop principle applied to organizational learning: the output (experience) must feed back into the system (knowledge base) to improve future action.

<div class="zh-trans">天合光能交流补充了制造业版本的闭环：知识指导角色 → 角色指导行动 → 行动产生复盘 → 提炼经验 → 形成知识 → 回到起点。与 OTGO 闭环原则完全同构：输出（经验）必须反馈回系统（知识库），才能改善未来行动。</div>
