# 品 (Taste) 哲学

## Definition

"品" (Taste / Connoisseurship) is OTGO's philosophical answer to the question: what is the irreplaceable human value in an AI age? It refers to **intuitive judgment derived from one's total accumulated life experience** — judgment that cannot be reached through logical deduction from given premises. This is distinct from "思" (inference/thinking), which is logical reasoning that AI can and should fully take over.

<div class="zh-trans">"品"（Taste/品鉴）是OTGO对"AI时代人的不可替代价值是什么"这一问题的哲学回答。它指**基于整个人生积累经验的直觉判断**——这种判断无法从给定前提通过逻辑推导得出。这与"思"（推理）不同，后者是AI能且应该完全接管的逻辑推理。</div>

## How It Works

**The 思/品 distinction:**
- **思 (inference)**: premises → logical deduction → conclusion. Reproducible, delegatable to AI.
- **品 (taste)**: no sufficient premises → judgment from life experience → "I want this." Example: Einstein's intuition that E=mc² before he could prove it. Steve Jobs deciding what the iPhone should feel like before any market research existed.

The existentialist framing from OTGO v12 Charter:
- *Cogito ergo sum* (I think) → superseded by AI
- *Ago ergo sum* (I act) → surviving: being present, creating context
- **Connoisseo ergo sum (I taste) → highest form, irreplaceable**

<div class="zh-trans">思/品区分：思（推理）：前提→逻辑推导→结论，可复现，可委托给AI。品（品味）：无充分前提→基于人生经验的判断→"我就要这个"。例子：爱因斯坦在能证明之前就直觉到E=mc²。Jobs在任何市场研究之前就知道iPhone应该是什么感觉。OTGO v12 Charter的存在主义框架：我思故我在→被AI取代；我行故我在→有生存空间；我品故我在→最高形态，不可替代。</div>

**Taste grows through action (the 卡点 theory):**
Taste (in the realm of ideals) and Context (in the real world) are always separated by a gap — called "卡点" (bottleneck/friction point). The gap represents the growth space. The right response to 卡点 is to go to the field and face it, not to analyze it from the office. "大卡大流" — bigger friction, bigger flow once breakthrough happens.

<div class="zh-trans">品通过行动增长（卡点理论）：品（在理念世界）与Context（在真实世界）之间总有落差，称为"卡点"。卡点代表成长空间。面对卡点的正确反应是去现场面对它，而不是在办公室分析它。"大卡大流"——卡点越大，突破后流量越大。</div>

**Warning — "using 思 to kill 品":**
Using rational arguments ("not mature yet," "ROI is unclear") to dissolve genuine intuitive desire is the most dangerous failure mode — it means using logical inference to negate intuitive judgment. Organizations that do this lose their 品 and become pure execution machines.

<div class="zh-trans">警惕"用思杀死品"：用"还不成熟""ROI不划算"等理性论证消解真实渴望，是用逻辑推理否定直觉判断。这样做的组织失去了品，变成纯粹的执行机器。</div>

## Why It Matters

This concept is the philosophical foundation of OTGO's entire organizational model:
- Why humans still have value in an AI-heavy organization (品 is irreplaceable)
- Why active field presence matters (品 grows through 行)
- Why openness in disagreement is required (unexpressed 品 = 失职)
- Why AI should never replace a person's independent action and judgment (that erases their existence)

<div class="zh-trans">这一概念是OTGO整个组织模型的哲学基础：为什么人在AI重度组织中仍有价值（品不可替代）；为什么主动在场很重要（品通过行增长）；为什么必须公开异议（未表达的品=失职）；为什么AI绝不能替代人的独立行动和判断（那会抹去他们的存在）。</div>
