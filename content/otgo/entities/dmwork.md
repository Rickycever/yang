# DMWork

## Overview

DMWork is an organizational communication analytics product by OTGO/明略科技. It measures team collaboration patterns using Pentland's three metrics (Energy / Engagement / Exploration) derived from MIT Human Dynamics Lab research. The product's strategic positioning: not a "team health dashboard" but an **organizational diagnostic tool** that maps communication patterns to specific business outcomes.

<div class="zh-trans">DMWork是OTGO/明略科技的组织沟通分析产品。基于MIT人类动力学实验室彭特兰三大指标（Energy/Engagement/Exploration）测量团队协作模式。产品战略定位：不是"团队健康仪表盘"，而是**组织诊断工具**，将沟通模式映射到具体的业务结果。</div>

## Role

DMWork's current status within OTGO: as of early 2026, 辉哥 has decided to **stop actively pushing DMWork** in favor of focusing on octo + 龙虾. DMWork is in a "hold" state until a v3.0 rewrite that reimagines it for the AI Native era. However, the sales playbook developed for DMWork remains relevant for future positioning.

<div class="zh-trans">DMWork在OTGO中的当前状态：截至2026年初，辉哥决定**停止主推DMWork**，专注于octo+龙虾。DMWork处于"暂停"状态，等待针对AI Native时代重新设计的3.0版本。但为DMWork开发的销售打法仍具参考价值。</div>

## Key Actions / Decisions

**Product insight**: The metrics are universal (apply to any industry) but the optimal configuration is industry-specific. This means the competitive moat is **industry-specific health baselines** — whoever accumulates baseline data across industries first wins.

**Sales strategy**: Never sell metrics — sell business problem answers:
- "You laid off 20%, why did profits drop?" → identify network hubs before cutting
- "30 hrs/week in meetings, projects delayed?" → find low-participation meetings
- "Merged two teams, still siloed?" → quantify cross-team connection gaps
- "Top sales left, revenue cliff?" → identify relationship network concentration risk

**Pricing path**: Free diagnostic (2-week data) → bind to business KPIs → monthly "organizational CT scan" as infrastructure.

<div class="zh-trans">产品洞见：指标通用，最优配置行业特定。竞争护城河是**行业特定健康基线**——谁先积累跨行业基线数据谁赢。销售策略：不卖指标，卖业务问题答案（裁员后利润反降、会议多项目延期、并购后未整合、销冠离职断崖）。定价路径：免费诊断→绑定业务KPI→月度"组织CT"成为基础设施。</div>
