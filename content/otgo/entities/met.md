# MET（电商后链路解决方案）

## Overview

MET is OTGO/明略科技's e-commerce post-link attribution solution. It measures the conversion effect of advertising on actual e-commerce sales (后链路 = post-click attribution), tracking OTT data, BDID data, OOH outdoor advertising data, and generating conversion analysis reports. Clients are major brands running multi-platform e-commerce campaigns.

<div class="zh-trans">MET是OTGO/明略科技的电商后链路归因解决方案。测量广告对实际电商销售的转化效果（后链路=点击后归因），追踪OTT数据、BDID数据、OOH户外广告数据，并生成转化分析报告。客户是在多平台电商运营大品牌。</div>

## Role

MET operates on a composite billing model: platform execution fee (¥15,000/execution/platform) + data processing fees + conversion analysis report (¥22,000). The core competitiveness challenge: the billing structure is perceived as expensive due to high fixed thresholds and additive multi-platform pricing.

<div class="zh-trans">MET采用复合计费模式：平台执行费（¥15,000/次/单平台）+数据处理费+转化分析报告（¥22,000）。核心竞争力挑战：由于固定门槛高和叠加式多平台计费，计费结构被感知为"太贵"。</div>

## Key Actions / Decisions

**Pricing evolution**: Between July 2025 and April 2026, MET moved toward finer billing granularity (per-account instead of per-platform) and made previously hidden costs (algorithm costs) explicit to clients — the trend increases total price even though unit prices appear unchanged.

**Competitive positioning gap**: Competitors offer year-bundle pricing or performance-based pricing. MET's per-execution + per-package + per-report model appears complex in comparison and invites line-item negotiation.

**Reform direction** (recommended):
1. Lightweight package at ¥20–25K to lower trial entry
2. Annual volume tiers to lock in large clients
3. Multi-platform cap (2× ceiling for 3+ platforms)
4. Remove or lower ¥50K minimum threshold
5. Add OOH intermediate pricing tier
6. Productize reports to reduce per-report labor cost

<div class="zh-trans">定价演变：2025年7月至2026年4月，MET向更细计费颗粒度转变（从按平台到按账号），并将隐性成本显性化——即使单价未变，总价实际更高。竞争定位差距：竞品提供年框打包价或效果付费；MET的逐项计费模式显得复杂，容易被逐项砍价。改革方向：轻量包降门槛、年框阶梯定价锁客、多平台封顶、取消最低门槛、OOH增加中间档、报告产品化降成本。</div>
