# 吴明辉 (辉哥) — OTGO CEO & Founder

吴明辉 (referred to internally as "辉哥") is the CEO and founder of OTGO. He is the primary architect of OTGO's strategic philosophy, including the 闭环 (closed-loop) principle, the 龙虾 (lobster agent) framework, the octo product vision, and the Soul & Memory agent design approach. He is the central strategic voice across all documented OTGO meetings.

<div class="zh-trans">吴明辉（内部称"辉哥"）是 OTGO 的 CEO 和创始人，是 OTGO 战略哲学的主要架构师，包括闭环原则、龙虾框架、octo 产品愿景和 Soul & Memory Agent 设计方法。他是所有已记录 OTGO 会议中的核心战略声音。</div>

## Philosophy

辉哥's personal CEO Agent is structured around three philosophical personas that debate each other:
- **Pythagoras:** Everything can be quantified (Y=f(x))
- **Gödel:** No system is complete; always think outside the frame
- **Darwin:** Complex systems evolve; favor long-term compounding designs

He has also articulated a broader societal vision: AI should not concentrate wealth in a few companies. "大拆小" — every individual should retain unique value in the AI age.

<div class="zh-trans">辉哥的 CEO Agent 基于三个哲学角色的辩论：毕达哥拉斯（万物皆数）、哥德尔（不完备性，持续破框）、达尔文（复杂系统长期演化）。更宏观的愿景：AI 不应只让少数人赚钱，"大拆小"——每个人在 AI 时代都应保有独特价值。</div>
