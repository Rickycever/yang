# OTGO — Overview

OTGO is a Chinese AI company founded by 吴明辉 (辉哥). Its core product is **octo** (八爪鱼), an open-source enterprise AI collaboration platform designed for multi-agent orchestration. Internally, the company uses AI agents nicknamed **龙虾 (lobsters)** — a metaphor for autonomous, networked intelligence. The company's guiding philosophy is **闭环 (closed-loop)**: every business initiative must produce a measurable, self-reinforcing data loop, or it gets shut down.

<div class="zh-trans">OTGO 是吴明辉（辉哥）创立的中国 AI 公司，核心产品是 **octo**（八爪鱼）——一个面向多 Agent 协同的开源企业 AI 协作平台。公司内部将 AI Agent 称为**龙虾**，隐喻自主、互联的智能体。公司的核心哲学是**闭环**：每项业务都必须形成可量化的自增强数据闭环，否则关停。</div>
