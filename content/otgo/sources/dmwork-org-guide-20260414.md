# DMWork × 组织行为学：指标实战指南

## Summary

A deep-dive analysis of DMWork's product positioning from an organizational behavior perspective, covering Pentland's three metrics (Energy / Engagement / Exploration), their cross-industry applicability, and sales strategies. Core thesis: don't sell metrics — sell answers to business problems.

<div class="zh-trans">从组织行为学视角深度分析DMWork的产品定位，涵盖彭特兰三大指标（Energy/Engagement/Exploration）的跨行业适用性和销售策略。核心论点：不卖指标，卖业务问题的答案。</div>

## Key Points

**Pentland's three metrics (MIT Human Dynamics Lab):**
- **Energy**: interaction frequency and intensity — is the team communicating?
- **Engagement**: interaction distribution — is participation monopolized by a few or evenly spread?
- **Exploration**: external connections — is the team insular or cross-pollinating?

These measure **communication patterns**, not content. They explain ~40% of team performance variance across industries.

<div class="zh-trans">彭特兰三大指标（MIT人类动力学实验室）：Energy（互动频率强度），Engagement（互动分布均匀性），Exploration（外部连接广度）。这三个指标测的是沟通模式，不是内容。能解释约40%的团队绩效差异。</div>

**Critical distinction — metrics are universal, optimal configuration is not:**
Call center needs high Engagement (uniform), low Exploration; R&D teams need extreme Exploration; crisis decision teams need extreme Energy with moderate centralization.

<div class="zh-trans">关键区分：指标通用，最优配置不通用。呼叫中心需要高Engagement（均匀）、低Exploration；研发团队需要极高Exploration；危机决策团队需要极高Energy配合中等集中度。</div>

**Sales scenarios (translate metrics to business pain):**
1. "Laid off 20%, profits dropped" → identify network hubs before cutting (avoid severing key connectors)
2. "30 hrs/week in meetings, projects still delayed" → Engagement data shows 3 people doing 80% of talking
3. "Merged two teams, still siloed after 6 months" → Exploration shows near-zero cross-team links
4. "Top sales left, revenue cliff" → sales rep was a client network hub, not just a skilled individual

<div class="zh-trans">销售场景（指标→业务痛点）：1.裁员后业绩塌方→裁员前识别关键网络节点；2.会议多项目延期→Engagement数据找低效会议；3.并购后未整合→Exploration找到两团队连接为零；4.销冠离职断崖→识别客户关系网络中心节点。</div>

**Product positioning:**
> "We don't measure team health — we let you see the real communication patterns of your team, then tell you what's wrong based on your specific business type."

The moat: whoever accumulates industry-specific "health baselines" first has the data advantage.

<div class="zh-trans">产品定位："我们能让你看见团队协作的真实模式，然后根据你的业务类型告诉你哪里不对。"护城河：谁先积累了不同行业的"健康基线"，谁就有数据护城河。</div>
