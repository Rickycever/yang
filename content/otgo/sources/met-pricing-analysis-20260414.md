# MET 电商后链路解决方案：报价分析

## Summary

Analysis of MET's e-commerce post-link (后链路) attribution solution pricing structure. Identifies three root causes of "too expensive" feedback from the sales team, and proposes six optimization directions focused on simplifying billing structure, lowering trial thresholds, and providing predictable cost caps for large clients.

<div class="zh-trans">分析MET电商后链路归因解决方案的定价结构。识别出业务团队反馈"太贵"的三个根本原因，提出六条优化方向，聚焦于简化计费结构、降低试用门槛、为大客户提供可预期的成本上限。</div>

## Key Points

**Current pricing structure (4 modules):**
- Platform + service fee: ¥15,000 per execution per platform
- Data processing: ¥600 per data package, ¥5/thousand for OTT translation, ¥0.12/thousand for BDID
- OOH outdoor data: ¥20,000–80,000 tiered by city scale
- Conversion analysis report: ¥22,000 per report
- Minimum discount: 70% (sales manager approval), lower requires VP/CEO approval

<div class="zh-trans">当前计费结构：平台服务费¥15,000/次/单电商平台；数据处理¥600/个数据包；OTT转译¥5/千个；BDID转译¥0.12/千条；OOH户外¥2-8万（城市分档）；转化分析报告¥22,000/个；最低折扣7折（销售经理审批）。</div>

**Why the sales team feels it's "expensive" — 3 root causes:**
1. **High fixed threshold**: ¥15,000 base + ¥50,000 minimum per execution — blocks small clients and pilots
2. **Multi-platform additive pricing**: 3 platforms = 1.4× multiplier on everything; large clients are most cost-sensitive
3. **Fragmented billing**: more line items = more negotiation points; perceived as complex and expensive vs. competitors' bundled pricing

<div class="zh-trans">"太贵"的三个根本原因：1.固定门槛高：¥15,000基础+¥5万单次门槛，挡住中小客户；2.多平台叠加计费：3个平台收1.4倍，大客户最敏感；3.费用项碎片化：越拆越细越好砍价，相比竞品打包价显得复杂。</div>

**Six optimization recommendations:**
1. Introduce ¥20,000–25,000 lightweight package (no deep report) to lower trial threshold
2. Annual volume discount tiers (10+ executions → ¥10,000/each; 20+ → ¥8,000/each)
3. Multi-platform bundle cap: 2 platforms 1.5×, 3+ platforms 2× ceiling
4. Remove or lower the ¥50,000 minimum execution threshold
5. Add OOH intermediate tier between ¥20K and ¥50K (e.g., ¥35K for 3–4 cities)
6. Productize reports as standardized templates + self-serve data access to reduce labor cost

<div class="zh-trans">六条优化建议：1.推出¥2-2.5万轻量包降低试用门槛；2.年框阶梯定价（10+次→¥1万/次；20+次→¥8千/次）；3.多平台打包封顶（双平台1.5倍，三平台及以上2倍上限）；4.取消或降低¥5万单次门槛；5.OOH增加¥3.5万中间档位；6.报告产品化（标准模板+自助取数）降低边际成本。</div>
