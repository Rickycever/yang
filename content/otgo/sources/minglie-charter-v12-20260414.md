# 明略组织行为准则 v12

## Summary

Not a standard employee handbook, but an **organizational philosophy manifesto + cadre behavior standard**. Its central question: when AI takes over "thinking," what is the basis for human existence in an organization? Answer: **行 (action)** and **品 (taste/connoisseurship)**. This document is the philosophical foundation for OTGO's AI Native organizational design.

<div class="zh-trans">这不是一般意义上的员工手册，而是一份**组织哲学宣言+干部行为标准**的混合体。核心问题：当AI接管了"思考"，人在组织里靠什么存在？答案：**行**（行动）和**品**（品鉴/Taste）。本文档是OTGO AI Native组织设计的哲学地基。</div>

## Key Points

**The three-tier existentialist framework:**
- *Cogito ergo sum* (I think, therefore I am) → logical inference → **already taken over by AI**
- *Ago ergo sum* (I act, therefore I am) → being present, taking action, creating context → **has survival space**
- *Connoisseo ergo sum* (I taste, therefore I am) → intuitive judgment from total life experience → **highest form, irreplaceable**

<div class="zh-trans">三重存在主义框架：我思故我在（逻辑推理）→ 已被AI接管；我行故我在（在场行动创造情境）→ 有生存空间；我品故我在（基于全部生命经验的直觉判断）→ 最高形态，不可替代。</div>

**思 vs 品 — the central distinction:**
- **思 (inference)**: given premises → logical deduction → conclusion. AI's strongest suit; should be fully delegated.
- **品 (taste)**: no sufficient premises → judgment from total life experience → "I want this." Human's irreplaceable value. Example: Einstein's E=mc² was taste before it was logic.

<div class="zh-trans">思（推理）：给定前提→逻辑推导→结论，AI的强项，应完全交给AI。品（品味）：没有充分前提→基于整个人生阅历的直觉→"我就要这个"，人的不可替代性。例子：爱因斯坦的E=mc²在成为逻辑之前，先是品。</div>

**Three first-principles of an AI Native organization:**
1. **Human-machine division**: humans do 行 and 品; machines do 思. Logical inference → AI Agent; humans acquire Context through real-world exploration and provide unique Taste.
2. **New collaboration**: use AI to exchange Context and Taste extremely efficiently, forming collective intelligence with no repetitive labor.
3. **Be good to people** (most important per 辉哥): AI's strength doesn't eliminate human value. "I want to help everyone protect their livelihoods."

<div class="zh-trans">AI Native组织三条第一性原理：1.人机分工：人做行和品，机器做思；2.新协作方式：极其高效地利用AI交换Context和Taste，不做重复性劳动；3.对人好（辉哥强调最重要）：AI再强大，不能把人干掉，要帮大家保护饭碗。</div>

**Core Team Charter (3 rules):**
- **C1**: Act in the company's interest with context — no context = no right to make decisions
- **C2**: Disagree openly and commit, or resign — silence is dereliction of duty; after discussion, execute fully
- **C3**: Be a committer — proactively raise conflicts (more important than resolving them)

<div class="zh-trans">Core Team Charter三条：C1——基于context、以公司利益行事；C2——公开异议后坚决执行，否则离开；C3——成为committer，主动raise冲突比解决冲突更重要。</div>

**Cadre standards (4 rules):**
1. Take ownership, deliver results
2. Worship action — be present, acquire Context, lead AI Agents
3. Highest standards — grind out High Taste through action (卡点理论: Taste gap = breakthrough space)
4. Activate team creativity — never use AI Agents to replace team members' independent action and taste

<div class="zh-trans">干部四条标准：1.担责达成目标；2.崇尚行动、在场获取Context、率领AI Agent干活；3.最高标准在行动中磨出High Taste（卡点=Taste与Context的落差，是突破空间）；4.激活团队创造力，绝不用AI Agent替代成员的独立行动和品鉴。</div>
