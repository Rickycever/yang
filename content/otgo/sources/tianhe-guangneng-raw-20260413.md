## Key Points

**AI in manufacturing — the core challenge:**
赵总 cited an MIT report that 95% of AI digitalization investments produce no effect. His company tried digitalization in paper manufacturing and ended up with 3× higher costs. The core insight: AI large models excel at language and reasoning, but cannot directly "work on the factory floor." Industrial production demands 100% accuracy — a 0.1% yield drop means major losses.

<div class="zh-trans">赵总引用MIT报告称95%的AI数字化投入没有效果。他们造纸行业的数字化案例成本反而高了三倍。核心判断：大语言模型擅长理解语言和思想，但不能直接在工厂干活。工业生产要求100%准确率——良率差0.1%就亏很多钱。</div>

**Scaling Out vs Scaling Up:**
辉哥's framework: large models are already a MoE (Mixture of Experts) structure under the hood. The right move is to decompose them into individual small models/Agents (龙虾), each handling a specific task — calling this **Mixture of Agents (MoA)**. Agent-to-agent communication becomes visible; each Agent's Memory and Skills are interpretable. Safety compliance is much stronger with small, transparent agents than with one giant opaque model.

<div class="zh-trans">辉哥的框架：大模型本质上是MoE（专家混合）结构。正确的做法是把它拆成一个个小模型/Agent（龙虾），每个做特定任务——称之为MoA（Mixture of Agents）。Agent间通信可见，每个Agent的Memory和Skills人都能看懂。小的透明Agent比一个大型不透明模型安全合规性强得多。</div>

**Knowledge management as prerequisite:**
赵总 proposed a three-tier knowledge framework — Know-What (conceptual), Know-How (procedural), Know-Why (root-cause, the "tacit knowledge" stored in master workers' heads). 辉哥 echoed this with Polanyi → Nonaka lineage and JTB epistemology. The deepest knowledge in manufacturing is tacit (暗默知识), which AI currently cannot extract but is the most critical for quality.

<div class="zh-trans">赵总提出三层知识框架：Know-What（概念知识）、Know-How（过程知识）、Know-Why（根因知识，存在老师傅脑子里的暗默知识）。辉哥呼应了波兰尼→野中郁次郎的知识管理脉络和JTB认识论。制造业最深层的知识是暗默知识，AI目前无法提取，却是质量的核心。</div>

**Lobster ethics & organizational change before tools:**
辉哥repeated his "龙虾商业伦理学": never use AI to eliminate people's jobs. Use AI to help people do things they couldn't do before. 赵总 confirmed: in his company, after digitalization, people finished work in 5 hours instead of 10 but spent the extra time on phones — because job responsibilities weren't redesigned. Conclusion: organizational change must precede tool deployment.

<div class="zh-trans">辉哥重申"龙虾商业伦理学"：绝不用AI消灭人的工作。用AI帮大家做原来干不了的活。赵总印证：他们公司数字化后，员工10小时的活5小时做完了，但多出来的时间在刷手机——因为岗位职责没有重新设计。结论：组织变革必须先于工具推广。</div>

**Token economics:**
辉哥's personal token spend: $5,000/week. The most active OTGO engineer: $8,000/day. OTGO's 2026 token budget: initially $100M, now estimated at $300–350M. DJI's benchmark: $1,000/person/month floor for all staff.

<div class="zh-trans">辉哥个人token消耗：一周5000美金。明略最猛工程师：一天8000美金。明略2026年token预算：年初定1亿，现在估计3-3.5亿美金。大疆基准：全员每人每月1000美金底线。</div>
