# 班委会周例会 · OTGO精神 (2026-03-09)

**Speaker:** 吴明辉 (辉哥, CEO) | **Date:** 2026-03-09 | **Format:** Weekly leadership meeting

This meeting established the foundational philosophy of OTGO's AI transformation. 辉哥 used a personal anecdote (revising his thesis with AI in 5 minutes vs. 3 hours) to assert that "the world has changed," then articulated three interconnected imperatives: full AI adoption (龙虾计划), closed-loop as the only business survival test, and octo as the next-generation infrastructure layer.

<div class="zh-trans">本次会议奠定了 OTGO AI 转型的核心哲学基础。辉哥用亲身经历（5分钟 vs 3小时改论文）宣布"世界已经变天"，并提出三条相互关联的核心命令：全面 AI 化（龙虾计划）、闭环作为业务生存的唯一标准、以及 octo 作为下一代基础设施。</div>

---

## Key Points

### 1. The World Has Changed — What Closed-Loop Means

The essence of closed-loop is: give AI **complete context** from the person who holds the real requirement. Humans in the middle who only relay information have no value. The human role is to have **taste** — to know what good looks like and to set goals.

<div class="zh-trans">闭环的本质是：把真正有需求的人的**完整 context** 输入给 AI，人不做传话者。人的价值在于有 **taste**——知道什么是好的，能够设定目标。</div>

> "这个世界真的已经变天了。如果我们每一个人只是一个传话者，我们在这个公司里面没有价值。我们每个人都要创造价值，要有 taste 的，知道什么是好的、什么是对的。"

### 2. 龙虾计划 — Company-Wide AI Agent Mandate

Three mandatory directives issued:
1. **All executives must train their own 龙虾.** Leadership (not frontline staff) controls *what* to do — that is the scarcest resource. Hardworking people pointed in the wrong direction cause harm.
2. **CEO will launch his own 龙虾** and open it to the whole company for strategy alignment.
3. **All information-relay work must go through octo** and be handled automatically by agents. Humans stop being middlemen.

<div class="zh-trans">三条强制指令：① 所有高管必须养自己的龙虾——管理层决定"干什么"，这才是最宝贵的；② CEO 本人上线自己的龙虾，开放给全公司做战略同频；③ 所有信息传递类工作必须通过 octo 由龙虾自动完成。</div>

> "我为什么没养龙虾？因为我怕我成为龙虾的瓶颈。我用不满它的算力。人类现在就是 AI 的瓶颈。"

### 3. Closed-Loop as the Only Business Standard

Businesses that cannot produce a closed data loop should be shut down immediately. This was validated by an IDG investor example: after a 2-hour discussion, the single deciding question was "can this business produce a closed loop?" — the answer was no, so they passed.

<div class="zh-trans">无法形成闭环的业务直接关停。IDG 案例：两位合伙人聊了两小时，最后只问了一个问题——"这个业务能产生闭环吗？"答案是不能，直接不投。</div>

**Examples of closeable loops:**
- E-commerce content: generates results on the spot ✅
- Short drama (traffic-based): AI distribution, quantifiable ✅
- octo custom dev outsourcing: client need → lobster develops → launch → client gets more capable → can't leave ✅

**Currently hard to close:**
- KV (brand visual identity): top-down enterprise decisions, long cycles, hard to quantify → workaround: use IDEF (content evaluation tool) to add a feedback node

### 4. octo — Next-Generation Infrastructure

octo does not compete with 飞书/企业微信 head-on. Instead, it enters existing social networks (飞书, 企业微信, 个人微信, 腾讯会议, etc.) as a "tentacle" — capturing context in real-time or near-real-time and feeding it into the agent network. Intelligence and compute accumulate on the octo side. Over time, octo becomes the core node; the others become distribution channels.

<div class="zh-trans">octo 不直接竞争飞书/企微，而是以"触角"方式进入人类现有社交网络，实时把 context 带入 Agent 网络。算力和智慧在 octo 侧积累，慢慢地 octo 成为核心节点，飞书/企微都变成它的渠道。</div>

> "AI 给我的建议：不要一上来旗帜鲜明说你要把他们全干掉。你应该先怂一点。"

**Commercial model:** Open source · custom dev service fee · token consumption × management multiplier

**Urgent missing capability:** Forwarding from 飞书/个人微信/企业微信 into octo — not yet built.

### 5. CEO Agent Architecture — Soul & Memory

辉哥's framework for building a CEO-level agent has two layers:

**Soul Layer (values/philosophy):** Three competing philosophical agents:
- Pythagoras: everything is number, Y=f(x) → quantitative thinking
- Gödel: incompleteness, always think outside the box → creative disruption
- Darwin: complex systems evolve over time → long-term systems thinking

These three spawn as separate sub-agents that debate each other. The answer that emerges after their collision is equivalent to deep personal reasoning.

<div class="zh-trans">CEO Agent 的两层架构：**Soul 层**——毕达哥拉斯（万物皆数）、哥德尔（不完备性，持续破框）、达尔文（复杂系统长期演化）三套哲学，各自 spawn 一个 sub-agent 互相博弈，碰撞后的结果才接近深度思考的水平。</div>

**Role Layer (function/context):** Strategy alignment · culture · product line sync · external communication

**Memory Isolation:** Four access tiers:
1. Highly confidential (CEO only)
2. Authorized visible (specific people/roles with read access)
3. Public
4. Write permissions controlled separately (strategy docs: read-only, cannot be modified by others)

> "这才叫 SOUL。每个龙虾是有自己灵魂的。所谓知行合一，就是这个人说什么、做什么，跟自己的底层逻辑完全 match。"

### 6. Organizational Transformation

Starting 2026-03-17: identify "transformation champions" among employees, bring them into the core collaboration network. Others are transferred or let go.

> "我还是有一个心愿，希望帮大家找到更好的工作，适应这个时代。"
