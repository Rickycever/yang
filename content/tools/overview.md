| Concept | [[tools/concepts/llm-wiki\|LLM Wiki 工具]] |

**Core idea**: Instead of RAG (retrieve at query time), maintain a persistent, compounding wiki where the LLM incrementally integrates new sources. The wiki accumulates synthesis and cross-references — knowledge compounds.

<div class="zh-trans">核心思想：不用RAG（查询时检索），而是维护一个持久化的复利Wiki，LLM增量整合新资料。Wiki积累综合分析和交叉引用——知识复利增长。</div>
